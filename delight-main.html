FAQ

Questions worth asking

Everything you need to know about deploying delight.ai in your organization.

What is an AI concierge?
An AI concierge is an AI agent trained on your knowledge base and past conversations to provide exemplary service autonomously — available 24/7 across every channel. Unlike generic chatbots, it builds a persistent understanding of each customer and represents your brand with nuance and care.
What use cases can it handle?
Any point in the customer lifecycle — from initial sales through ongoing support, plus proactive re-engagement and renewal campaigns. Common use cases include order management, appointment scheduling, product discovery, loyalty program management, and first-line technical support.
Can it improve operational efficiency?
Yes. It handles routine tasks like rescheduling appointments, order updates, local recommendations, and proactive outreach — freeing your team for high-value interactions. Customers report an average 40% reduction in support volume within the first 90 days.
How do you monitor and improve it?
Built-in Watchtower system monitors adherence to your Actionbooks (standard operating procedures) with live performance metrics and AI-generated improvement insights. Every conversation is scored and surfaced for review, with suggested prompt improvements automatically generated.
