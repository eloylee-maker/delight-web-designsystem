FAQ

Questions worth asking.

The questions we'd want answered if we were evaluating vendors.

Is Sierra just an AI layer on my existing helpdesk?
Yes — Sierra's architecture is designed to sit on top of your existing helpdesk (Zendesk, Salesforce, etc.) rather than replace it. That means faster initial deployment, but it also means AI and ticketing are never fully unified. Data silos, context loss between systems, and integration maintenance are ongoing realities. Delight.ai ships with a native helpdesk so the AI and the CX workflow operate from a single source of truth.
Can delight.ai replace our existing helpdesk entirely?
Yes. Delight.ai ships with a fully functional native helpdesk — ticketing, routing, agent workspace, reporting, and the AI layer are all integrated and share the same data model. Most enterprise customers migrate within 4–8 weeks. For teams that want to keep an existing system during transition, we support parallel operation with a defined migration timeline.
How does AI governance work in delight.ai?
Trust OS is our response-level governance layer that evaluates every AI output before it reaches the customer. It checks for safety violations, brand tone, regulatory constraints, and factual grounding against your knowledge base. CX and compliance teams can configure guardrails themselves through a no-code interface — no engineering tickets, no vendor professional services required. Every decision is logged for audit.
How long does a delight.ai deployment take?
For a standard enterprise deployment — AI agent live on chat and email, helpdesk migrated, Trust OS configured — we target 4–6 weeks from contract to go-live. Complex multi-channel or regulated-industry deployments typically run 8–12 weeks. We've done same-day proofs of concept for qualified prospects.
