Grid foundationscanvas, columns, gutter, offset

Usage ruleswhen to bleed, when to snap

RuleDetail
Snap to columnsSection headers, button rows, form labels, and feature cards must align to the 16-col grid (PC). No sub-column placement.
Full-bleedHero images, GNB, and footer can extend to the 1920 canvas edge. Inner content still snaps to 1552 / 16 cols.
Span rangesPC headlines: 8–10 cols. Body copy: 6–8 cols. Feature cards: 4 cols (×3 across). FAQ: 8 cols centered.
GutterAlways 16px across breakpoints. Never collapse, never widen.
OffsetPC 184px, Tablet 40px, Mobile 16px. Do not adjust per page — it's a system constant.
