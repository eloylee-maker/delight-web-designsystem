Global Navigation Bar (GNB)

The GNB is required on every delight.ai page. It uses position: fixed with a 72px .gnb-spacer directly after — never position: sticky. Scroll down to see the bottom border appear, and click the menu items to open dropdowns.

Anatomy
• .gnb-announcement — optional 57px black bar above (scrolls away)
• .gnb-wrap — fixed positioning, contains everything sticky
• .gnb-spacer — required, height 72px, immediately after wrap
• .gnb.scrolled — adds bottom border when window scrollY > 0
• Capabilities = mega menu (1265px) • Solutions = 500px panel • Resources = 260px panel
• Lang & Logo switcher = small popovers (.gnb-drop-pop)
