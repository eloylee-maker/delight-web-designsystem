Component / Navigation

GNB

Top navigation bar — 72px tall, position: fixed. PC shows the full menu, mega-menu, and right-side utilities. On mobile (≤900px) the menu and utilities collapse into a hamburger drawer.

PC ≥ 900px · full menu + dropdowns
Mobile 375px · hamburger + drawer

Mobile behavior

  • At ≤900px the inline menu (Capabilities / Solutions / Resources / Customers) and right-side utilities (EN / Log in) hide.
  • A hamburger button appears in the right cluster — tap to slide a full-screen drawer down from below the bar.
  • Drawer rows: 4 menu sections (chevron-right), then EN / Log in side-by-side, then a full-width Black "Contact sales" CTA.
  • The 42px Contact sales pill in the bar shrinks to 14px font on mobile so it still fits.
  • Tap the X (hamburger toggles to close icon) or any drawer row to dismiss.
Anatomy
• Bar: 72px tall, position: fixed, white bg, 72px spacer reserves layout space
• Logo switcher: 74×50 pill (--bg-subtle) → popover with delight.ai / Sendbird (44px square icon tiles)
• Menu: Helvetica Now Text 16/500, 24px gap, full-bar height; active state = 2px black underline
• Capabilities mega: 1265px wide, two link cols (300px each) + promo region (2 cards + 8 integration icons)
• Solutions panel: 500px, two cols (Industries / Use Cases)
• Resources panel: 532px, two cols (Learning · Insights+Tools)
• Right cluster: globe + EN, person + Log in (gap 8, no divider), Black 42px Contact-sales CTA
• Scroll: hairline border under bar past 10px scroll; open dropdowns close past 20px scroll
