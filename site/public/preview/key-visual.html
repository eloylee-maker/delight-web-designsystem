03
Image + mockup UI

Establish a clear visual hierarchy so image and UI don't compete. The image provides mood and context; the UI or diagram delivers the key information. Mockup overlays use 12 px backdrop-blur and the standard drop shadow.

Specs
Canvas
1920 × 1080 (16:9). Source images cropped at cover; bleeds beyond the frame are intentional.
Base layer
Photographic source over a #292016 backplate. Imagery is warm, motion-blurred, natural — never cold or blue-cast.
Scrim
rgba(0,0,0,0.20) uniform overlay. Lifts to 0.35 when copy needs to sit on top.
Noise
Tileable grain at opacity 0.4, mix-blend-mode overlay. Non-negotiable — adds the brand's characteristic softened-print texture.
Mockup overlay
White surface, 92% opacity, backdrop-filter: blur(12px), radius 14 px, drop shadow 01.
Mockup-only palette
Background uses --bg-subtle (#F7F5F0) as bg1 and a warm gradient as bg2. No other colors permitted.
Approved subject matter
Trees, water, shadows, light, fabric, hands, interior moments. Photography page is dominated by warm interiors and lifestyle — no cold/blue palettes.
