FAQ Accordion
닫힘: gray border 원+Plus / 열림: Lime bg 원+Minus. 반드시 두 SVG 토글 방식 사용.
What makes delight.ai different from other AI platforms?
delight.ai is built on a persistent Agent Memory Platform that creates a living record of every customer interaction — enabling truly personalized AI experiences at enterprise scale.
How does the Trust OS ensure responsible AI?
Trust OS monitors every AI interaction in real-time, flagging hallucinations and off-brand responses before they reach customers.
Can delight.ai integrate with my existing tools?
Yes. delight.ai integrates natively with Salesforce, Zendesk, Freshworks, Intercom, and dozens more CX platforms.
